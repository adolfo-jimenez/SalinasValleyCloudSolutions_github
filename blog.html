Website development still on going

August 28th, 2021

Making quite a bit of progress with this website. Most of the main pages were created except for the Services page and Shortcuts pages but the current pages that have been added to the Salinas Valley Cloud Solutions website so far are very basic, at least for now. I plan on adding more design and . . . content as I work on this project but that I suppose goes without saying.

I'm starting to get more ideas as I surf the web more to look at other website designs for inspiration. I can see that other sites use a lot of Javascript on the front end to make their pages look more stylish and responsive. Once I begin filling in the webpages with enough content, I'm going to begin focusing more on Javascript in general to know the ins and outs of that programming language
